Frequently Asked Questions

Who is Robert Pattinson?

Robert Pattinson is a British actor and musician best known for his roles in the "Twilight" film series as Edward Cullen and as the titular character in "The Batman" (2022).

When was Robert Pattinson born?

Robert Pattinson was born on May 13, 1986, in London, England.

What is Robert Pattinson's full name?

His full name is Robert Douglas Thomas Pattinson.

What are some of Robert Pattinson's notable films?

Some of his notable films include "Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire" (2005), the "Twilight" series (2008-2012), "Remember Me" (2010), "Water for Elephants" (2011), "The Rover" (2014), "Good Time" (2017), "The Lighthouse" (2019), "Tenet" (2020), and "The Batman" (2022).

How did Robert Pattinson start his acting career?

Pattinson began his career in theater before landing his first major film role as Cedric Diggory in "Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire" (2005).
